Espresso-Based Drinks

Espresso
A strong, concentrated shot of coffee, forming the base for many other drinks. Loved by purists.
Best Dessert for Espresso
Tiramisu
The classic Italian dessert with coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone perfectly complements espresso's bold flavor.
Dark Chocolate Truffles
The bitterness of dark chocolate balances the intense espresso shot.

"Sweet dreams are made of sugar and cream."

Latte
A creamy blend of espresso and steamed milk, often topped with a light layer of foam. Customizable with flavors like vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut.
Best Dessert for Latte
Cheese Cake
The creamy texture and mild sweetness pair well with the smoothness of a latte.
Cinnamon Rolls
The warm spice of cinnamon enhances the latte’s creamy taste.

"Treat yorself, you deserve it."

Cappuccino
Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, known for its rich texture and balanced flavor.
Best Dessert for Cappuccino
Croissant
A buttery, flaky croissant complements the rich and foamy cappuccino.
Macaron
These delicate, sweet cookies pair beautifully with cappuccino's balanced flavor.

"Love at first bite."

Americano
Espresso diluted with hot water, giving it a similar strength but a milder flavor than black coffee.
Best Dessert for Americano
Apple Pie
The mild flavor of an Americano pairs well with the sweet and tart notes of apple pie.
Shortbread Cookies
Their buttery simplicity complements the clean, bold coffee.

"Dessert: the ultimate love language."

Flat White
Originating in Australia/New Zealand, this drink features espresso with a velvety layer of steamed milk, smoother than a latte.
Best Dessert for Flat White
Pecan Tart
The nutty and caramel flavors harmonize with the smooth flat white.
Banana Bread
Moist and slightly sweet, this pairs wonderfully with the creamy coffee.

"Indulge in sweet life."
